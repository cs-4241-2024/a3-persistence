Tea Log

Welcome! Here you can keep track of the cups of tea you have dranken. Just enter the type (ex. Black, Green, Earl Grey), the date (ex. Sunday, 9/8/24), and your rating (0-10). The server then generates a meaning based on the rating. Identify the row you want to delete by its date and type fields. Identify the row you want to edit by its date and type fields, then choose what you want to edit
